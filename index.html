ACHAAR GHOSHT

RECIPE BY CHEF FARAH

INGREDIENTS

Beef boneless 500 kg
Mehran mixed pickle 3 spoon
Mehran garlic paste 3 spoon
Mehran ginger paste 2 spoon
Mehran chilli powder 1-2 tbsp
Mehran turmeric powder 2tbsp
Tomato slice cutting(6)
Cooking oil 4 tbsp
Water as needed
Onion slice cutting (2)
Salt as per taste
Cumin powder 1 tbsp
Coriander crushed 1 tbsp
Green chilli 4
Yogurt half cup
Butter 1 tbsp
